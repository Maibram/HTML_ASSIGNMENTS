Shop is a shopping destination and delivery tracking app that's available to customers on iOS, Android, and on the web. Customers can use the Shop app to track packages, discover new stores and products, make purchases using Shop Pay, and engage with your brand.

Shop Pay is an accelerated checkout option designed to enhance the shopping experience for customers on your Shopify store and on Shop. It allows customers to save their email address, credit card, and shipping and billing information, making the checkout process faster and more convenient.
